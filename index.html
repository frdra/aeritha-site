Questions

Good to Know

How does self check-in work?

After booking, you'll receive a unique digital access code via email, SMS, or WhatsApp 24 hours before arrival. Simply enter the code on your room's smart lock — no front desk, no waiting. Takes under 30 seconds.

Is there staff on-site if I need help?

Yes. While check-in is automated, a small on-site team of 2–3 staff handles housekeeping, maintenance, pool bar service, and any personal assistance you may need.

What makes Aeritha different from a regular budget hotel?

Unlike typical budget hotels, every aspect of Aeritha is intentionally designed — from the brand identity to room interiors. We deliver boutique-quality design at budget-friendly prices by using technology to reduce operational overhead, not by cutting corners on your experience.

Can I check in at any time?

Absolutely. Our digital access system works 24/7. Whether your flight lands at midnight or sunrise, your room is always ready for you.

Do I need to download an app?

No app download required. Your access code works directly on the smart lock. Everything you need is delivered via standard messaging channels like email, SMS, or WhatsApp.

Is the rooftop pool included in my stay?

Yes, the rooftop infinity pool is complimentary for all guests. The poolside bar offers drinks and light bites at additional cost.
